About The Series

The Wheel of Time is a high fantasy book series created by Robert Jordan. The series is known for it's huge size. It includes 14 volumes - each consists of 826 pages on average! Fantasy lovers say it only gets better as you read more!

From The Critics

Jordan writes with the stark vision of light and darkness, and sometimes childish sense of wonder, that permeates J.R.R. Tolkien's works. His style is undebatably his own.

— Pittsburgh Press

Those who like fantasy can rejoice. This is the genuine article.

— John Lee, author of "The Unicorn Solution"

A great read... Some surprising new developments... I can't recommend starting anywhere but at the beginning, but the volumes only get richer as they go along.

— Locus

James Oliver Rigney Jr.

American epic fantasy writer known better by his pen name - Robert Jordan. Born in 1948 in Charleston, South California U.S.. Dropped out of high school after a year and enlisted in the U.S. Army. After returning to U.S. Jordan studied physics at college, which he graduated in 1974 and began working as nuclear engineer for the U.S. Navy. He began writing in 1977.
